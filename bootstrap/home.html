Computer Assisted Music Generation System

This is a system that was developed in order to help people compose music regardless of their background and training in either music or computer science.  It has been developed as part of the Senior Thesis Project at Allegheny College.  It is free to use.

This tool was developed to guide a user through the process of creating a melody.  The musical ideas are yours, but the system provides a novel method of input and guidance to allow anyone to successfully create music.
